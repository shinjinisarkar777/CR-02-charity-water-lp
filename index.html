Our Story

charity: water began in 2006 with a simple mission: bring clean and safe drinking water to people in need around the world. Our founder, Scott Harrison, saw firsthand the impact of dirty water while traveling and wanted to make a difference. Since then, charity: water has funded thousands of water projects in 29 countries, helping over 17 million people. We believe in 100% transparency—every dollar donated goes directly to water projects, and every completed project is shown on a map with photos and GPS coordinates. Together, we can end the water crisis.
